PTSD Monitoring App

An iOS application that collects biofeedback data to monitor PTSD levels in veterans

Timeframe: February to May 2020

Team: Grace Fan, Gannon Prudhomme, Arjun Rao

Skills: Product design, user research, low to high-fidelity prototyping

Tools: Figma

My Role

I revised existing app designs and introduced new features based on user research provided by the lab faculty. I used Figma to develop high-fidelity prototypes and worked closely with Gannon (lead iOS developer) to translate designs into code.
