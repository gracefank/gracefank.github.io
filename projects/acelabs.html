Team: Gabriel Britain, Grace Fan, Sibo Min, Sandesh Neupane, Sahil Palnitkar, Deshantar Parajuli

Skills: Project management, user stories, user research, low to high fidelity prototyping

Tools: Figma, HTML/CSS (Bootstrap, SASS)/JavaScript, Ruby on Rails

My Role

I also served as the scrum master and product owner, communicating with our customer, Professor Paul Taele.
