Overview

2D sidescroller game developed in 48 hours between three team members in October 2017.

Challenges

The premise of the game was to create a whimsical interpretation of the theme “role-reversal.” In the game, the cat and fish relationship is reversed and the player plays as a cat swimming away from a villanous fish. I designed the art seen in the game and wrote code to handle character interaction.

Approach

I created both idle animations and event animations for the characters by illustrating each frame in Adobe Illustrator.

I chose to draw in a cartoon-like style to best convey the feel of the game, including exaggerated features and movement.

Collisions between the enemies and the player are vital to the gameplay. I had to use rigid body characters and tags to separate different collision situations.

To create a scrolling effect, I implemented a sideways animation using multiple layers of waves and overlapping them.

To increase the difficulty of the game, we decided to create power-ups that have different effects upon collision.
