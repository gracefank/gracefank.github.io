Me in 30 Seconds

Hi, I'm Grace and I'm always looking to talk about new products and designs. I have experience both as a front-end developer and as a UX designer. My passion for both code and design stems from a drive for problem-solving and a love for working with users to create accessible and human-centered products.

I recently received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Texas A&M University and am currently working as a front-end developer at IBM. Formerly, I interned at Credera and Sandia National Labs and was the Creative Director at TAMUhack, one of the largest student-run hackathons in Texas.

Outside of work, I relax by playing the ukulele and decorating my 5-star Animal Crossing island 🌴
